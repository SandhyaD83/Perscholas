Global Focus

The Boeing Company is the world’s largest aerospace company and leading manufacturer of commercial jetliners and defense, space and security systems. Boeing is committed to being the best in aerospace and an enduring global industrial champion—a top performer in each and every area of our business delivering superior value to customers, employees, shareholders, communities and partners.

Boeing’s global reach includes customers in approximately 150 countries and employees and operations in more than 65 countries. The company has manufacturing, service and technology partnerships with companies and governments worldwide and contracts with more than 20,000 diverse suppliers and partners.

Boeing is the United States’ largest exporter and works closely with its many partners and suppliers around the world to create the greatest aerospace products. Today, more than 70 percent of Boeing’s total backlog is represented by customers outside the U.S. Our business growth benefits local partners, customers, shareholders and employees globally. Our increasing in-market presence and local partnerships in key global markets are expanding our business even more. Partnerships with the world’s best companies and research institutes enable us to capture emerging opportunities, establish long-term relationships and win new business.

Global business drives Boeing’s growth and secures our leadership position now and in and future.

              

Boeing International

The Boeing International organization is responsible for the company’s international strategy and corporate operations outside the U.S., overseeing 18 regional offices in key global markets. The organization develops the company’s growth and productivity initiatives outside the U.S., forming new business and industrial partnerships, overseeing international affairs, enhancing Boeing’s local presence and providing global functional support.
